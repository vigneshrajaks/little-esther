WHO’S ESTHER?

Esther is a shy little girl who’s afraid to show her feelings. Instead, she likes to hold a balloon of many kinds to express her mood and thoughts. If it’s a sunny day and Esther is feeling awesome, she would love to bring out her ice cream balloon for a stroll; if Esther’s upset, she would hold her rain cloud balloon and let the feeling go away with the rain.
